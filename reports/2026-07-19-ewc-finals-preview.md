The exploitable seam is **side selection**: KC is 14-5 on blue but 5-6 on red, while
DK is comfortable on both and actually *better* on red (75%). Over five games, KC
must win red-side games; DK doesn't have that problem. Draft will be tense in the
overlap: both teams lean on Lucian+Milio, Annie, and the Jarvan/Vi jungle pool, and
KC's signature Rumble (8 games, 88% WR) has already eaten 5 targeted bans this event.

**Prediction: Dplus Kia 3-2 (~55/45).** DK's macro edge and side flexibility should
decide a long series. The upset case is real: KC's comeback vs T1 showed they don't
fold from behind, and if Rumble/Gnar priority survives draft, blue-side KC wins fast games.
